How VoteSecure Works

A step-by-step guide to using our secure voting platform

1

Register as a Voter

Create your account with your personal details and constituency information.

  • Provide your full name and email address
  • Select your constituency
  • Verify your email with OTP
2

Verify Your Identity

Complete the email verification process to ensure account security.

  • Check your email for verification code
  • Enter the 6-digit OTP
  • Account activated immediately after verification
3

View Active Elections

Access your personalized dashboard with elections in your constituency.

  • See all ongoing elections
  • View election details and candidates
  • Check voting deadlines
4

Cast Your Vote

Select your preferred candidate and submit your vote securely.

  • Review candidate profiles
  • Make your selection
  • Confirm your vote (cannot be changed)
5

View Results

See election results after the voting period ends.

  • Real-time result updates
  • Detailed statistics and analytics
  • Receive result notifications

Security Features

OTP Verification

One-time passwords ensure only authorized voters can access the system.

AI Fraud Detection

Advanced algorithms detect and prevent suspicious activities.

Secure Encryption

All data is encrypted to protect voter privacy and election integrity.
